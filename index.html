Hello There!!

My name is Christina and I am a professional freelance makeup artist located in Toronto with several years of experience. My specialty lies in providing full make-up services for all occasions and ages including parties, weddings, engagements, prom, graduation, photo-shoots and special events. I have attended a Humber College for Cosmetic Management program and have graduated with distinction among my peers. I have always been passionate about inner and outer beauty and have pursued following my dream of being a make-up artist since a young age. I am an evident follower of all sorts of beauty related YouTube channels and news. I am always excited and happy to share my experience and skills with people who are like-minded as me.

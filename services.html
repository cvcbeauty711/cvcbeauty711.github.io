Services

  • Make-Up Application Services

    Don’t feel like doing your own makeup? I've got you covered! Whether you want to feel more beautiful on your big day, or for a fun night out, I offer full makeup services so you can focus more on finding that perfect outfit! Full-face makeup application services typically last anywhere from 45 to 60 minutes per person, depending on the complexity of the look.

I am able to do any makeup look from dramatic smokey eyes to soft feminine glam and everything in between, so whether you’ve chosen a dewy glowing finish or a matte and flawless finish, together we will put your best face forward by enhancing your natural beauty on your special day.

  • Safe & Right Make-Up Teaching Services

    Offering a customized 90-minute makeup lesson, specifically designed with YOUR personal questions and concerns in mind. With my unique instruction, and using my formula for finding the perfect safe and beautiful combinations for everyone, we teach you how to do your makeup by factoring in eye color, skin tone, skin type, hair color, age, and so much more!

    Experience a guided tutorial where I walk you through a proper and safe step-by-step full face makeup application, including the following: Daytime/Office Eye Look, Basic Smoky Eye Look, Every Day Complexion, Every Day Contouring, Find Your Perfect Lip, Skincare Recommendations, Product Recommendations, Proper Brush/Tool Usage, etc. I only use non-toxic organic products for this class as I am all about sharing the safe and ethical make-up practices to fellow beauty admirers.

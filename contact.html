About Me and What I Do

I teach YOU how to become your own makeup artist! I can use my products or your products from home whichever works best for YOU! Already have a handle on your own products? No problem! I also teach numerous tips, tricks, and techniques, and provide you with a customized Product Recommendation List according to your OWN personal attributes, so that you have the option to add to your makeup collection... Or not! The choice is yours.The BEST part? It's a PARTY! I strongly believe having a make-up party is the perfect way to have a girl's night, whether it is to get to know your co-workers better, celebrate a special occasion, or just hang out with your friends, all while enhancing your makeup skills!
